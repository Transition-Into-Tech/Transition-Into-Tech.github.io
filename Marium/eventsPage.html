International themed launch

We launched our bank on Tuesday the 6th of December. After many meetings of preperation, our launch day couldn't have gone any better. There were many students eager to take an application form and recieve a treat. We announced the winner for our Suzy in a Suitcase Competition. We took the two classes before lunch to set up in the foyer outside the art room. We put together the treats we had for the people who would take an application form. We put out tables and covered it with red table cloths. We set out tables with the application forms on one side of the foyer and the cakes and treats on the other side. We all then dressed up in holiday themed clothes. We had Austrailian, Texan and Hawaiin themed clothes on. As soon as the bell rang, we got a swarm of students. During the majority of lunch we had to give out application forms.
