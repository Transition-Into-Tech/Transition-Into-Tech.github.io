Design is more about building up a living environment, rather than decorating rooms. Lighting is an amazing quality which can transform the appearance of a room, without doing anything else to the space involved.I think that using colour in a confident way brings out the colour in a room also not being afraid to use bold colours, but it must always be used with an eye to orientation and function. I feel that an interior has to work first and then look well. To achieve this goal I spend much time considering the function required of the choosen space.If you are looking for an Interior in which the space flows, the lighting brings the room to life, with classy materials that are not easily identifiable, contact me. My experience enables me to find solutions to your problems easily and affordable.
