TY Work Experiences




This website is all about Transition Year work experiences. I did five work experiences in total throughout the year which will stay with me for the rest of my life. They have been the highlight of TY for me. On this website you can find a daily blog on each experience, my day to day work, duties and challenges that I encountered over the week. It will also outline the skills that I learned and improved on and my reflection on the overall work experience. I will also upload pictures taken on each experience.
